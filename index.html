About Us

The University of Otagooro is a leading institution of higher learning, offering a diverse range of programs in arts, sciences, and engineering. Our faculty and staff are dedicated to providing students with a well-rounded education and a supportive environment for personal and academic growth.

Programs

  • Bachelor of Arts in English
  • Bachelor of Science in Computer Science
  • Bachelor of Engineering in Electrical Engineering

Admission

Admission to the University of Otagooro is competitive and based on academic merit, extracurricular activities, and letters of recommendation. To apply, please submit your transcript, SAT/ACT scores, and a personal statement. For more information, visit our Admission page.
